Notes & Methodology

01

Selection Criteria

Briefings are selected based on their likely consequence for semiconductor supply chains, technology trade, or geopolitical positioning relevant to the semiconductor ecosystem. Not all press briefings are included — only those with material relevance.

02

India Angle

Each entry includes my own analysis of how the development affects India's strategic interests — as a semiconductor ambition, a technology consumer, a diplomatic actor, or a potential supply chain node. This is interpretive, not official.

03

How I Track

I monitor RSS feeds and press release portals from MEA, PMO, White House, State Department, MFA China, MOFCOM, MIIT, and others. Items are filtered for semiconductor and technology geopolitics relevance before being added.
