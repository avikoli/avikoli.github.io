Hi, I am Avinash Koli, a technology policy and strategy professional. Currently, I steer technology policy and geostrategy for the CEO's Office, Tata Electronics.

Previously, I was part of the Strategic Support Unit (SSU) at the CEO Office, Anusandhan National Research Foundation (ANRF), Government of India, closely supporting the CEO across process and programme reforms.

Apart from my role at ANRF, I worked as a programme manager with Foundation for Advancing Science and Technology (FAST India), a S&T policy think tank based in Delhi, studying models of technology and innovation to suggest programmatic interventions for clients.

Recently, I was engaged as a NAST Fellow (2024–25) with the Takshashila Institution. My working paper developed during the fellowship was conferred with the Best Paper Award.
