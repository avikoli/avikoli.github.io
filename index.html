About

◻ Your Photo

Policy thinker. Industry practitioner. Global perspective.

I am a technology policy and geopolitics expert currently working in the semiconductor industry. My work sits at the crossroads of industrial strategy, international relations, and emerging technology governance.

Over the past decade, I have advised governments, think tanks, and private sector leaders on how to navigate an increasingly fragmented technological landscape — from export controls and supply chain resilience to the geopolitics of chip manufacturing.

I believe that sound policy requires deep technical literacy, and that the decisions made in semiconductor boardrooms today will shape the balance of power for the next generation.
